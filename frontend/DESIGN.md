# Design System: University Hunt (Cliento Inspired)
**Project ID:** university-hunt-v2

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere
The design follows an **Airy Editorial** aesthetic. It is defined by vast amounts of whitespace, a soft "Cloudy Sky" background, and a sophisticated balance between premium serif typography and clean functional sans-serif elements. The vibe is professional yet approachable, conveying a sense of clarity and elevated education.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles
*   **Sky Mist (#F8FBFE):** The primary background color, evoking a sense of calm and openness.
*   **Sunset Orange (#FF7A2E):** The primary accent color used for Call-to-Action buttons and interactive highlights.
*   **Midnight Charcoal (#1A1A1A):** Used for primary headings and body text to ensure maximum readability and contrast.
*   **Soft Cloud White (#FFFFFF):** Used for card backgrounds and elevated components with glassmorphism effects.
*   **Muted Steel (#64748B):** Used for secondary text, labels, and inactive states.

## 3. Typography Rules
*   **Primary Heading (Serif):** Uses a high-contrast editorial serif (e.g., 'Instrument Serif' or 'Playfair Display'). Used for Hero titles and section headers. Characterized by tight letter spacing and large font sizes.
*   **Body & UI (Sans-Serif):** Uses a clean, modern sans-serif (e.g., 'Inter' or 'Outfit'). Used for navigation, descriptions, and functional UI elements. Focus on legibility and generous line-height.

## 4. Component Stylings
*   **Buttons:** 
    *   Primary: Dark Charcoal background with a distinctive Sunset Orange square icon at the end (pill-shaped).
    *   Secondary: Sunset Orange pill-shaped buttons with white text.
*   **Cards/Containers:** "Subtly rounded" (8-12px) with whisper-soft diffused shadows. Use glassmorphism (white/90 + backdrop-blur) for a premium feel.
*   **Navigation:** Minimalist, centered links with balanced padding. Sticky header with soft glassmorphism.
*   **Logo Cloud:** Greyscale/Desaturated versions of partner logos at the bottom of the page to provide social proof without visual clutter.

## 5. Layout Principles
*   **Whitespace:** Extremely generous margins and padding to create a "breathable" interface.
*   **Alignment:** Centered hero section for maximum impact, grid-based listings for search results.
*   **Rhythm:** Clear vertical hierarchy with distinct sections separated by soft transitions or subtle borders.
